The Vital Importance of Coral Reefs

Coral reefs, known as the rainforests of the sea, support 25% of all marine species. These vibrant ecosystems are under threat from climate change, pollution, and overfishing. Protecting coral reefs through sustainable practices and conservation efforts is essential for maintaining marine biodiversity and the livelihoods of millions who depend on them.
